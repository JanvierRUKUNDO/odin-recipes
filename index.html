Weeknight Wellington

Weeknight Wellington is a quick and straightforward take on the traditional Beef Wellington that's ideal for hectic weeknights..

Ingredients

  • Beef fillet or steak
  • Puff pastry sheets
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic
  • Fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil or butte

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the temperature specified by the puff pastry package instructions.
  2. Season the beef fillet with salt and pepper on all sides.
  3. n a skillet, heat some olive oil or butter over medium-high heat. Sear the beef fillet on all sides until browned. Remove the fillet from the skillet and set it aside to cool.
  4. In the same skillet, add a little more oil or butter if needed, then sauté the sliced mushrooms, chopped onion, minced garlic, and fresh thyme until the mushrooms have softened and released their moisture. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  5. Roll out the puff pastry sheets on a lightly floured surface. If needed, you can join two sheets together to create a larger piece to wrap the fillet.
  6. Spread a thin layer of Dijon mustard over the puff pastry sheet(s), leaving a border around the edges.
  7. Spread the sautéed mushroom mixture evenly over the prosciutto or ham layer
  8. Place the cooled beef fillet on top of the mushroom mixture

Peanut Butter Cookies

Peanut butter cookies are delicious, soft and chewy treats made with the rich and nutty flavor of peanut butter.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Unsalted butter
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified in the recipe (typically around 350°F or 180°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the peanut butter to the butter-sugar mixture and mix until well combined.
  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the bowl, and continue mixing until the wet ingredients are thoroughly incorporated.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in tough cookies.
Air Fryer Honey Garlic Chicken Wings

Air Fryer Honey Garlic Chicken Wings

Air Fryer Honey Garlic Chicken Wings are crispy and flavorful chicken wings coated in a sweet and savory honey garlic glaze, cooked to perfection in an air fryer.

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Garlic cloves
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Sesame oil
  • apple cider vinegar
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine minced garlic, honey, soy sauce, ketchup, rice vinegar or apple cider vinegar, sesame oil, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create the honey garlic sauce.
  2. Place the chicken wings in a separate bowl and season them with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken wings in the preheated air fryer basket, making sure they are not overcrowded. Cook in batches if necessary.
  4. Add the egg and vanilla extract to the bowl, and continue mixing until the wet ingredients are thoroughly incorporated.
  5. Set the air fryer to cook the wings for approximately 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through. Flip or shake the wings halfway through cooking to ensure even browning.
  6. Once the chicken wings are cooked, transfer them to a large mixing bowl.
